Stithi

APU Undergraduate Math Club




Welcome to the webpage of Stithi, the undergradate mathematics club of APU. We are a group of students and professors interested in mathematics,and wish to explore the aspects of the vast and varied discipline. We aim to make the most of it by arranging regular discussions and presentations about different and interesting topics.

Please write to us about anything you find at, Stithi, that is worth discussing or/and presenting in the club.

The club is meant to be a forum for a bunch of activities. We will be discussing the history of mathematics, mathematical literature, explore different regions of mathematics and discuss about them. Sometimes, we might also have problem solving and discussion sessions, in order to hone our understanding of certain concepts through problems, and also may play relaxing games like solving puzzles and riddles. We will also be hosting talks by students and professors on novel and hopefully, engaging themes. Overall, we aspire to be a platform for the mathematically inclined to refine and study areas of their interest, and non-mathematical to discover and enjoy the subject.
